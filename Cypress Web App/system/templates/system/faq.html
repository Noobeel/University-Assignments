FAQ
What does Cypress stand for and what is its purpose?

City of Toronto Problem Reporting and Solution System.

CYPRESS is built for people who value their environment and want to make it a better and safer place for others. The Cypress system is designed to allow citizens to report problems that concern them about their city that they notice on the streets.

How do I contact Cypress?

On our portal page, there is a link labelled Contact Us. Click on it and you will be directed to a page with our contact details. You can choose from the provided contact details on how you would like to reach us.

Click here for contact details.

How do I tell if the problem I have reported has been completed?

You will be required to subscribe to completion notification when submitting a report. CYPRESS will notify you when the city completes your report through the email address provided.

I accidentally submitted a report with the wrong address. Can I edit a submitted report?

Make sure you are logged in to the account used to create the report. On the portal page, click on 'Update My Reports'. A new page with all your reports will open up. Click on the edit button below each report to edit that report. Once a report is completed, it cannot be edited.

When will work on my submitted report start?

Please allow up to 5 business days for the city to fully process your report.

I would like to delete my report. How would I do that?

Make sure you are logged in to the account used to create the report. On the portal page, click on 'Update My Reports'. A new page with all your reports will open up. Click on the delete button below each report to delete that report. You will be prompted to confirm deletion of that report. Once a report is completed, it cannot be deleted.

Can I report a problem outside Toronto?

Unfortunately, no. The CYPRESS system is designed only for the residents of Toronto.

Can I delete my profile?

Yes, you can. Login to your account and click the 'Profile Information' button on the portal page. You will be redirected to your profile page where you can edit your personal information or profile picture. Additionally, you can also delete your profile.

How do I report a problem on CYPRESS?

Login to your account or register for one if you haven't already. On the portal page, click on 'Report A Problem'. You will be redirected to the report page where you can click on an interactive map to specify the location of the problem. You will also be prompted to select a problem from a number of options and to subscribe to email notifications.

If the problem I need to report is not listed among the options provided on the report page, what should I do?

Please find our contact information on the Contact Us page. You can send us the problem through email or you can go to the nearest City of Toronto office to report a problem.
